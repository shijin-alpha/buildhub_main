  2. Find a project with "Accepted Project" status badge
  3. Click the green "üì§ Upload Design" button in the action buttons
  4. Technical Details Modal opens with comprehensive form sections
  5. Fill out construction details (foundation, structure, materials)
  6. Specify technical specifications (dimensions, heights, thicknesses)
  7. Configure electrical and plumbing requirements
  8. Enter cost estimates and construction timeline
  9. Select materials and finishes preferences
  10. Add special features and construction notes
  11. Click "Submit to Homeowner" to send complete design package
  12. Homeowner receives design with technical details for review
